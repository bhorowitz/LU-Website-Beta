Herng Yi was a member of the MIT Educational Studies Program, which organized Splash and other programs. He served as its representative to LU for a year, and since then has steadily become more involved with LU. He also co-founded a Splash-like program Sprout in his alma mater, NUS High School in Singapore. Herng Yi is a mathematics major but his interests span theater, origami, open access to scholarly research, science journalism, art, music and more. He is passionate about exploring the self and the world, and helping people find their passions!
